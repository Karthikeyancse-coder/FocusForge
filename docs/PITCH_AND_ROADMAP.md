# FocusForge AI - Pitch & Roadmap

## 1. Hackathon Pitch Deck Outline
- **Slide 1: Title Screen** - "FocusForge AI: Your productivity becomes a living story."
- **Slide 2: The Problem** - Students miss deadlines and burn out on soulless checklists.
- **Slide 3: The Solution** - Productivity combined with high-fidelity gamification & AI triage.
- **Slide 4: AI Prioritization** - How our custom algorithm detects High-Risk tasks automatically.
- **Slide 5: Gamification** - Character progression, Boss Battles, Streak Shields.
- **Slide 6: Architecture** - React, Tailwind, Framer Motion, Firebase.
- **Slide 7: Why We Win** - This isn't a checklist. It's a premium SaaS behavioral tool.

## 2. Competitive Analysis
- **Todoist/Notion**: Incredible for list-making, zero intrinsic motivation.
- **Habitica**: Good gamification, but chaotic UI, feels like a retro 8-bit game, not a clean modern SaaS.
- **FocusForge**: Merges the clean, calm UX of productivity stalwarts (Linear/Arc) with visually striking premium gamification. 

## 3. Investor Pitch Summary
"We are building the future of educational technology by merging behavioral psychology, LLM-based scheduling agents, and premium gaming mechanics to fix the student engagement crisis."

## 4. MVP Roadmap
- **Phase 1 (Hackathon MVP)**: Core UI, Dashboard, Task CRUD + AI priority mock algorithms, single character class loop, light/dark mode CSS tokens.
- **Phase 2 (Post Hackathon)**: Full Firebase integration, 3D React Three Fiber Character models.
- **Phase 3 (Scale)**: Guilds, advanced stats.

## 5. Future Scope
- Custom 3D study rooms.
- Voice assistant "AI Coach".
